**Simplified user journey:**
1. Leal wakes up and weighs himself on his Withings scale
2. Leal goes to [oracles.fit](http://oracles.fit) and signs off his results onchain. Already logged in with Farcaster, read-access to Withings granted
3. Leal casts into /fitness to be eligible for the "Get Fit" round on [rounds.wtf](http://rounds.wtf)
4. Automatic rewards distribution through Rounds.wtf
